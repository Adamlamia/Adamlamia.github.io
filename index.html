Story

I received an invitation from the Tokyo government to participate in a global discussion camp at Tokyo Metropolitan University focused on the Tokyo Olympics 2021. The theme was centered around the role of volunteerism among youth.

During the week-long event, I had the opportunity to interact with fellow volunteers of the Tokyo Olympic event, conduct interviews at the Volunteering Center, and engage with representatives from the Tokyo government. This experience led to significant personal achievements and the establishment of valuable friendships and professional connections.

Right after my short-term exchange program at TMU, I seized the opportunity to participate in a long-term exchange program at Korea Maritime and Ocean University.

This new venture enabled me to delve into various aspects of Korean language and history, explore historical sites, examine Korea's advancements in fishery and aquatic environments, delve into the logistics of import and export, and gain insights into the country's marine ecosystem. My travels took me to multiple locations across the nation, including Pohang, Ullong Island, Seoul, and Mokpo.
